Sea Turtle Facts

Here are some basic facts about sea turtles.

Sea turtles, are reptiles that live in the waters around the U.S., Australia, Papua New Guinea and Indonesia. Sea turtles are herbivors and eat mostly seagrass.

There are seven species of sea turtles which are:

  • green sea turtle
  • loggerhead sea turtle
  • Kemp's ridley sea turtle
  • olive ridley sea turtle
  • hawksbill sea turtle
  • flatback sea turtle
  • leatherback sea turtle
